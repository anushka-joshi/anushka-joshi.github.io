Brief Bio

I am the recipient of the prestigious Prime Minister Research Fellowship at the forefront of computer science research, working on advanced algorithms and applications. My research focuses on quantum computing, artificial intelligence, and their intersections in real-world domains. I hold a PhD from IIT Roorkee and M.Tech from IIT Hyderabad. I completed my B.Tech at G.B Pant Institute. I am passionate about teaching and mentoring students.
